PotionPanic

Null Forge Studio | PC | Co-op time-management crafting game

Role: Networking Engineer

Summary

PotionPanic is a chaotic, score-driven apothecary game where players race the clock to fulfill potion requests. The core loop combines gathering ingredients, processing them at specialized stations, brewing recipes, and delivering finished potions for points while avoiding penalties from mistakes or expired orders.

What I Worked On

  • Served as the Networking Engineer and collaborated closely with the full team to build and integrate multiplayer systems.
  • Implemented RPC and replication workflows for gameplay features, UI synchronization, and game pausing behavior.
  • Built session flow systems for creating, finding, joining, and destroying multiplayer sessions.
  • Implemented host settings and invite-to-game functionality to support smoother lobby and match setup.
  • Integrated Steam online services to create listen servers and handle player joins reliably.
  • Built and refined friends list filtering to group players by In-Game, Online, and Offline states.
